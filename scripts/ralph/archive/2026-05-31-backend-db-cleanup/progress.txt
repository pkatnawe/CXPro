## Codebase Patterns
- asyncpg pool lives on `app.state.pool` (set in lifespan in backend/main.py)
- `get_conn` in backend/db.py is the only DB dependency; `get_db_connection()` has been deleted (US-005)
- `get_conn` resolves credentials via `Depends(HTTPBearer())` and lazily imports `get_current_user` from auth.py to avoid circular imports (auth.py imports db.py for get_supabase_client)
- Python compile/syntax check: `python -m py_compile <file>`; existing tests: `python -m pytest test_main.py -x -q`
